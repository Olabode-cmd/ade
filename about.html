More About Ade

  • Dr. Ade Akinrinola is a life & career coach, an education consultant, speaker, and tech leader.
  • He holds a Ph.D. in Education Policy, Organization and Leadership from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign and a master’s degree from Michigan State University.
  • He has more than 10 years of experience in education consulting and leadership, life and career coaching, speaking, tech leadership, and academic research.
  • As a higher education professional who has worked at 3 R1 universities, including Michigan State University, University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, and The University of Texas at Austin, and served in various leadership capacities within the Comparative and International Education Society (CIES), Dr. Ade incorporates his expertise and praxis in the global higher education landscape into the quality of consultancy he provides to clients.
  • He has been invited to speak at a variety of professional and faith-based events in Africa, South and North America, including the Comparative and International Education Society (CIES), The International Learning & Assessment Rethinking (I-LEARN) Summit, and Living Spring State College Community Church.
  • As director of curriculum & learning experience design at AltSchool Africa, Dr. Ade directs education strategy, forges institutional partnerships and leads curriculum development, implementation, and evaluation by leveraging research and data to identify key strategies for improving the quality of education and learning experience of students at AltSchool Africa.
